About Tajikistan

Tajikistan is a mountainous country in Central Asia with rich traditions, kind people, and breathtaking landscapes. It shares borders with Afghanistan, China, Kyrgyzstan, and Uzbekistan.

Quick Facts:

  • Capital: Dushanbe
  • Official language: Tajik
  • Population: Around 10 million
  • Main Mountains: Pamir and Fann Mountains
  • Main River: Amu Darya
